// three steps

Set It Up Once. Then Just Write.

From install to finished song in minutes.

1

Get Your Free API Key

Sign up at openrouter.ai, paste your key into Settings. Free models available — no credit card required.

2

Tell It What You Want

Pick a genre, mood, topic. Name an artist for style influence. Hit Generate. That's it.

3

Write Your Song

The AI writes line by line. Refine, regenerate, lock the lines you love. Export the full song when you're done.
